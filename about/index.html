About

2025-09-15 1 min read Page Views

I hold a master’s degree in Chemical Engineering and Technology from Chongqing University of Technology. During my graduate studies, my research on microbial diversity sparked my interest in bioinformatics. As the first student in my lab to explore multi-omics bioinformatics analysis, I had to teach myself everything from the ground up, which was both challenging and rewarding. Although my programming background was limited at first, I gradually built up my skills through persistent self-study.

I was fortunate to gain practical experience through an internship at Wuxi YiO Biotechnology Co., Ltd., where I received detailed guidance from my mentor, Dr. Rong Zhengqin. This experience greatly enhanced my abilities in bioinformatics analysis and gave me the confidence to continue advancing in this field.
